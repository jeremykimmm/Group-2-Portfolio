Title: The School for Good and Evil

Year: 2022
Genre: American fantasy film

Director: Paul Feig
Screenplay: Paul Feig and David Magee, based on the 2013 novel by Soman Chainani.

Starring: Sophia Anne Caruso (as Sophie) and Sofia Wylie (as Agatha), leading an ensemble cast.

Synopsis: Two best friends, Sophie and Agatha, are sent to the Schools for Good and Evil. When their fortunes are seemingly reversed, their friendship is put to the test.

Development & Production: The film's development started in 2013 with Universal Pictures, but it languished in development hell. Netflix took over in 2017, and Feig was hired in 2020. Principal photography took place in Northern Ireland between January and July 2021.

Release & Reception: Released on Netflix on October 19, 2022. The film received generally negative reviews from critics, who criticized the narrative and storytelling, but it received praise for its cast and visuals.
