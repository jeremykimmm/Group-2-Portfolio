Title: White Chicks (stylized as WHiTE CHiCKS)

Year: 2004
Genre: American buddy cop action comedy film

Director: Keenen Ivory Wayans
Writers: Keenen Ivory Wayans, Xavier Cook, Andy McElfresh, Michael Anthony Snowden (screenplay co-written); Marlon Wayans and Shawn Wayans (additional contributions).

Starring: Marlon Wayans, Shawn Wayans, Jaime King, Frankie Faison, Lochlyn Munro, and John Heard.

Synopsis: Two black FBI agent brothers go undercover as white women using elaborate whiteface prosthetics to protect two hotel heiresses from a kidnapping plot targeting socialites.

Filming Locations: Chilliwack and Victoria in British Columbia, and The Hamptons in New York.

Reception: Released on June 23, 2004. The film received generally negative reviews upon release and was nominated for five Golden Raspberry Awards, including Worst Picture. Despite this, it was a commercial success, grossing over $113.1 million worldwide, and has since been regarded as a cult classic.
