Title: School of Rock

Year: 2003
Genre: American comedy film

Director: Richard Linklater
Writer: Mike White
Producer: Scott Rudin

Starring: Jack Black, Joan Cusack, Mike White, and Sarah Silverman.

Synopsis: Jack Black plays Dewey Finn, a struggling rock guitarist who gets fired from his band. He poses as a substitute teacher at a prestigious prep school and, after discovering the students' musical talent, forms a band of fifth-graders to win the upcoming Battle of the Bands and pay his rent.

Box Office: Released on October 3, 2003, the film grossed $131 million worldwide on a $35 million budget. It was the highest-grossing music-themed comedy until 2015.

Adaptations: The film received positive reviews, particularly for Black's performance and humor. It was adapted into a stage musical that opened on Broadway in December 2015, and a television adaptation that aired on Nickelodeon from 2016 to 2018.
