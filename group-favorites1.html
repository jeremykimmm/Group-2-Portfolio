Title: Fear Street Part One: 1994 (titled onscreen as Fear Street 1994)

Year: 2021
Genre: American supernatural slasher film

Director: Leigh Janiak
Writers: Phil Graziadei and Leigh Janiak (from a story by Kyle Killen, Graziadei, and Janiak), based on the book series by R. L. Stine.

Synopsis: The film follows a teen and her friends after a series of brutal slayings, as they take on an evil force that has plagued their notorious town for centuries.

Starring: Kiana Madeira, Olivia Scott Welch, Benjamin Flores Jr., Julia Rehwald, Fred Hechinger, Ashley Zukerman, Darrell Britt-Gibson, and Maya Hawke.

Development & Release:
Development began in 1997 but never materialized. It re-entered development at 20th Century Fox in 2015. Filming for the trilogy took place back-to-back from March to September 2019 in Georgia. Originally planned for a June 2020 theatrical release, it was pulled due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Distribution rights were given to **Netflix** in August 2020 following the Disney acquisition of Fox.

Premiere & Release Date: Premiered at the Los Angeles State Historic Park on June 28, 2021, and was released on Netflix on July 2, 2021. It received generally positive reviews.
